Why Text Justification Matters

Text justification ensures that the paragraph aligns with both the left and right margins, creating a clean and professional look. This is especially useful for content-rich websites like blogs, news articles, and formal reports. It enhances readability by maintaining visual balance and consistency across lines.

However, it's important to use justification wisely, as it can sometimes introduce awkward spacing between words. Tailwind CSS makes it easy to apply justification using the text-justify class.
